## Shared Open Chain

Most generated documents converge on this boundary:

1. A role screen builds a `PdfDocumentDescriptor`.
2. `src/lib/documents/pdfDocumentActions.ts` runs preview/session orchestration.
3. `src/lib/pdf/pdf.runner.ts` and `src/lib/pdfRunner.ts` normalize generated HTML, local URIs, remote URLs, share/open handoff, and viewer state.
4. `app/pdf-viewer.tsx` opens a prepared local/remote source. It is not the root cause for paths where source aggregation or render/upload starts on click.

Canonical remote assertions already exist for `foreman:request`, `director:director_report`, `director:supplier_summary`, and warehouse document/register/material PDFs.
